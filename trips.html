Ethiopia

Make your way from the city of Mekele to a small town called Wukro. From here you will meet with one of our contacts that will organise transport to the church of Abuna Yemata Guh. A guide on site can to take you to the top of the mountain to meet the priest who allows access inside the rock-hewn church. This involves a steep, unharnessed and barefoot climb to reach the top. You will also visit the monastery of Debre Damo. The entrance of which requires a 15m rope climb. Price: €400. Length: 5 days.

India

Rent one of our petrol rickshaws in Chennai and race through southern India to reach the city of Alappuzha. How are your navigational skills? You will have no set route or daily plan. Manuever through the busy streets and struggle up the mountains in a glorified lawn mower. This road trip is only for strong willed and independent travellers. Rickshaw fits a maximum of 3 people. Price: €980. Length: 10 days.

Mongolia

Stay with a nomadic family in the heart of Mongolia. You wil live in close quarters inside a traditional Mongolian Yurt. Experience day to day life with the family, particpating in activities such as caring for livestock, preparing local authentic food, farming and horse trekking. Learn how to live a more minimilastic, eco-friendly and simple life. Price: €350 Length: 7 days.

Mauritania

Ride out from the capital city of Nouakchott to a small settlement in the Sahara desert. Here you will wait patiently for an iron-ore train that passes only twice a day. This train is one of the longest in the world, at 2.5km long. It will stop here for less than 10 minutes, giving you an opportunity to climb aboard. Riding on top of the carts is seen as right of passage for the locals. Take a cart and chug through the sahara desert for 450km till you reach the final destination, the city of Nouadibou. This journey will last through the night so we hope you're not a light sleeper. Price: €250. Length: 3 days.
